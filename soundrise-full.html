TERMS & CONDITIONS

Step 4 of 4 — Please read and accept

1. ELIGIBILITY

You must be 18 years or older to use SOUNDRISE. By registering, you confirm that the date of birth you provided is accurate. Accounts found to belong to users under 18 will be permanently terminated.

2. COMMUNITY RULES

SOUNDRISE is built on mutual respect and genuine engagement. You agree to engage honestly with other creators' music. False reporting of engagements is strictly prohibited and will result in strikes and account bans.

3. ENGAGEMENT SYSTEM

The 60-second timer system is designed to ensure genuine listening. Attempting to circumvent the timer, using bots, or engaging fraudulently will result in immediate account termination. You may only engage with each creator's content once per day through this platform.

4. STRIKE SYSTEM

3 strikes = 24-hour upload ban. 6 strikes = 72-hour upload ban. 10 strikes = 1-week ban. 13 strikes = permanent account termination. Strikes are issued for false engagement reports confirmed by our verification system.

5. CONTENT OWNERSHIP

You retain full ownership of your music. By posting links on SOUNDRISE, you confirm you own or have rights to the content linked. SOUNDRISE does not host music files — only links to external platforms.

6. MUSIC LINKS

Only YouTube and Spotify links are accepted. Links must point to your specific song or track, not your channel or profile homepage. Invalid or misleading links will result in content removal.

7. DATA PROTECTION

Your personal data (name, email, date of birth) is stored securely and encrypted. We comply with GDPR requirements. Your data is never sold to third parties. You may request deletion of your account and data at any time by contacting support.

8. LEADERBOARDS

Weekly and daily leaderboards display your username and engagement counts publicly within the platform. By joining, you consent to this visibility.

9. ACCOUNT TERMINATION

SOUNDRISE reserves the right to terminate accounts that violate these terms. Terminated accounts forfeit all accumulated visibility points.

10. CHANGES TO TERMS

These terms may be updated. Continued use of SOUNDRISE constitutes acceptance of updated terms. You will be notified of significant changes.
